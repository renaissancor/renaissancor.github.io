    Quick Sort Algorithm

    Randomized Quick Sort

    Quick Sort is based on Divide and Conquer. Compared to other sorting mechanisms with same time complexity of \(O(n log n)\) including heap sort and merge sort, quick sort has way much better performance due to its high cache hit probability by using two pointers linearly.

    However, the weakness of Quick Sort is when pivot selection makes the worst choice in sequence. For instance, if one array is sorted in descending order, and the goal is to re sort it again in ascending order, and pivot selection is sequential from 0 to n or vise versa, then the pivot value selected might be the biggest or the smallest in order, so that It will move pivot only to the edge of the index, resulting in time complexity of \(O(n^2)\) in the worst case.

    However, application of random selection of pivot might be useful alternative.
